How I work

What you can expect.

A few things I take seriously every time I work with someone.

🎯

I come prepared.

I use a discovery call to understand your setup before I arrive. You won't have to explain everything twice, and I won't show up and figure it out as I go.

💬

I speak plain English.

No jargon. No condescension. I meet you at your level, whether you're a first-time smartphone user or a seasoned professional with a complex setup.

🤝

I teach, not just fix.

My goal isn't to be indispensable. I want you to feel confident and capable when we're done. I'll always explain what I'm doing and why.

🔍

I'm honest about scope.

If something is outside my wheelhouse, I'll say so and point you in the right direction. I don't overpromise or take on work I can't do well.

⚡

I don't waste your time.

I show up on time, work efficiently, and give you clear next steps before we wrap up. No open-ended sessions that drag on without a resolution.

🏡

I'm local and accountable.

This is my business and my reputation. Every client is a referral source for the next one. That keeps me accountable in a way a large company never has to be.
